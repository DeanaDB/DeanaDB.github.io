Strange But True: Nature’s Ultimate Team-Ups!

Ever heard of a fish that gets its teeth cleaned by another fish or a bird that hitches a ride on a rhino’s back? Welcome to the wild world of symbiosis, where living things team up in surprising ways to survive and thrive. In this lesson, you'll explore real-life relationships in nature that range from helpful to harmful nand everything in between. Get ready to dive into ecosystems and discover how creatures connect, cooperate, and compete!

Lesson 1

Symbiosis: Commensalism

“I take, but I don’t hurt. I share space, but not the rewards. Who am I in nature?”

Learning Objective:
Given a set of ecosystem scenarios and images, the student will be able to identify and explain examples of the symbiotic relationship of commensalism in nature with 80% accuracy.
