Meet Mrs. Bartholomew

Hi, I’m Mrs. Bartholomew!
I have been a middle school science teacher for 16 years. I love helping students ask big questions and discover how the world works. Learning and teaching are two of my favorite things. I believe it’s important to stay curious and appreciate the little things in life. Over the years, teaching middle school students has brought so many fun and meaningful experiences in the classroom. I am so excited to learn and grow with you this year!

I am married to my best friend. We have two awesome sons who keep life exciting and full of laughter. When I am not teaching, I love being outdoors enjoying nature, spending quality time with my family, or reading a good book.
